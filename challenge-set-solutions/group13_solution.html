Question 4

Email data suggests that 45% of all email is spam. A common phrase that shows up in Spam is "Act Now". It was found that 10% of all spam contained that phrase. Conversely, 2% of all non-spam contained that phrase. What is the probability that an email is Spam if it contains the phrase "Act Now"?

Answer:

  • P(Spam)=0.45P(\text{Spam}) = 0.45P(Spam)=0.45
  • P(¬Spam)=0.55P(\neg \text{Spam}) = 0.55P(¬Spam)=0.55
  • P(Act Now∣Spam)=0.1P(\text{Act Now} \mid {Spam}) = 0.1P(Act Now∣Spam)=0.1
  • P(Act Now∣¬Spam)=0.02P(\text{Act Now} \mid \neg {Spam}) = 0.02P(Act Now∣¬Spam)=0.02
  • P(Spam∣Act Now)=0.1∗0.450.1∗0.45+0.02∗0.55≈80%P(\text{Spam} \mid \text{Act Now}) = \frac{0.1 * 0.45}{0.1 * 0.45 + 0.02 * 0.55} \approx 80\%P(Spam∣Act Now)=0.1∗0.45+0.02∗0.550.1∗0.45​≈80%
